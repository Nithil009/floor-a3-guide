Section 01

Kitchen Rules

⭐
Golden Rule
The kitchen must be kept extremely clean at all times. Clean up immediately after yourself — do not leave anything behind.
❌
No dishes left in the drying area overnight — and no "quick parking" on the counter. The 5€ rule is in effect: unidentified items left behind = 5€ fee.
❌
No wet vessels in the shelves. Dry everything with the dish cloth before putting it away. Moisture damages the shelves.
❌
Never leave the sink clogged after washing dishes. Clean the drain every time.
❌
Never leave food or items in the kitchen after you're done eating. Take your food back to your room.
⚠️
If you break a glass — stop, carefully clean all shards, and inform the group chat immediately so others know to be careful.

Section 02

Waste Sorting

Please sort waste carefully and place it in the correct bin.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Everyone's job
When throwing waste in the kitchen, put it in the right labelled bin. Sort at the point of disposal — not later.
🗑️
Waste duty person
When a bin is full, take it to the dumping yard and empty it into the correct container. Bring your transponder. Also empty the washroom bins.
📹
There is a camera in the dumping yard. Wrong disposal can lead to fines. Always sort correctly.

Which bin for what?

🟤
Bio Waste
Food scraps, peels, coffee grounds, eggshells
🗑️ Bio container
🟡
Plastic & Metal
Packaging, cans, foil, tins
🗑️ Wertstoff container
⚫
Restmüll
Everything else — mixed non-recyclable waste
🗑️ Restmüll container
🍾
Glass
Bottles and glass jars
🗑️ Outside ~15 steps
📦
Cardboard
Flatten boxes before disposing
🗑️ Outside ~15 steps
📍
Where are the containers?
Bio, Plastic & Restmüll are in the dumping yard (basement — needs transponder). Glass & Cardboard bins are outside, ~15 steps from the dumping yard entrance.

Section 03

Weekly Duties

Each duty rotates weekly via a door sign. It's a simple system — just follow the sign.

1
Sign appears on your door
2
Read the back for instructions
3
Complete the duty this week
4
Pass the sign to the next room
🏖️
Going away for 2+ days? Put the "Absent" sign on your door so the duty skips you that week.

What each duty involves

🗑️
Waste Duty — Empty all dustbins on the floor — including kitchen and washroom bins — and take bags to the dumping yard. Sort into the correct containers. Bring your transponder! Leave bins empty and clean.
📹Note: Camera in the yard. Wrong disposal = fines. Sort carefully every time.
🛒
Shopping Duty — Check the board checklist. If any shared item (e.g. salt, sponges) is below the required quantity (usually 2–3 packs), buy it. Keep your receipt — the finance handler will reimburse you.
🧺
Kitchen Cloth Washing — Collect used kitchen cloths from the used tub in the living room and wash them in the washing machine. Once clean and dry, place them back in the fresh tub. There are 3 types: hand towel, dish-drying cloth, and surface cloth — refresh all three.
🧺

Section 05

Bathrooms & Toilets

We have 2 shared washrooms and 2 shared bathrooms. Keep them clean for everyone.

🚿
After your shower — rinse the shower area using the pipe/hose provided. Wipe away any hair, soap residue or gunk. Leave it as you found it (or better).
🚽
Toilet flush tip: The flush isn't very strong. If needed, use the bucket nearby as a power flush — fill with water and pour in forcefully to push everything through.
❌
Never leave the toilet clogged without informing the group. Write in the group chat if there's a blockage you can't fix.
🚰
Keep the shower pipe facing INSIDE
After your shower, use the pipe/hose inside the bathroom to rinse the floor clean. Always make sure the nozzle faces inward into the bathroom. If left pointing toward the open area and accidentally turned on, water will flood the middle area — there is no drain there to handle it.

Important Notes

💶 The 5€ Rule

💶
Kitchen items left unattended = 5€ fee
If you leave your dishes or belongings in the kitchen without a good reason and the owner can be identified, a 5€ fine applies. This rule was introduced because things escalated badly in the past. Just wash and put away your stuff — it takes 3 minutes.

💬 Group Chat Etiquette

💬
Report broken items, clogged toilets or anything that needs fixing in the group chat immediately.
🤝
Need to borrow something? Just ask in the group — we're all here to help each other.
📣
Announce large gatherings in the group beforehand so your floormates can plan their cooking.
😊
We're a friendly floor. Don't be afraid to knock on doors or ask questions — everyone was new once.
